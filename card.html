Tabby Cat

Tabby cats often have a distinctive M-shaped marking on their foreheads.

  • Scientific name: Felis catus
  • Coat pattern: Classic orange tabby
  • Average weight: 3.5–5 kg
  • Average lifespan: 12–15 years

Friendly and curious, orange tabby cats are known for their sociable temperaments. They are affectionate companions who thrive in calm indoor spaces and enjoy interactive play that taps into their natural hunting instincts.
